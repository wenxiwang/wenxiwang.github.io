I am a fourth year PhD student at Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) Department, in The University of Texas at Austin, supervised by [Prof. Sarfraz Khurshid](https://users.ece.utexas.edu/~khurshid/). I am also closely working with [Prof. Kenneth Mcmillan](http://mcmil.net/wordpress/). My interests mainly focus on Constraint Solving, SAT/SMT solving, Model Counting, Software Testing, and Machine Learning including Neural Networks.

During my Masters, I was working with [Prof. Peter Stuckey](https://people.eng.unimelb.edu.au/pstuckey/) and [Prof. Harald Sondergaard](https://people.eng.unimelb.edu.au/harald/) for a word-level bit-vector SMT solver called Wombit, at School of Computing and Information Systems, The University of Melbourne.

**Services**: PLDI '21 Artifact Evaluation Committee Member.
